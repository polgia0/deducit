Score Contribution Object to Average


  The score contribution  plot is  required when we would like to understand why one object is far away from the center of the plane (i.e. from the average of variables). Basically, this plot splits the  score of one object in the contribution due to the variables. The object can by chosen by the slider . Of course, it is also necessary to fix which latent variable (score) to analyses by the left combo box. In the next figure I selected the first object  in the direction of Component 1.


The resulting plot is a bar plot where the weight of each variable is shown. Higher is a bar on a variable, higher is the difference of variable vs the average of all the other objects. This  highlights why an object can be an outlier in the Data Set.
